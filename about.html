About Magnificent Furnitures Limited

Your trusted partner for quality furniture in Kenya since 2015

Our Story

Magnificent Furnitures Limited was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, affordable furniture to homes and businesses across Kenya. What started as a small showroom in Nairobi has grown into one of the country's most trusted furniture providers.

Over the years, we have built strong relationships with manufacturers and craftsmen who share our commitment to quality. Every piece of furniture we sell is carefully selected for its durability, design, and value.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of customers — from individual homeowners to large corporations, schools, and government institutions. Our extensive product range includes over 500 furniture models across 17 categories, ensuring we have something for every need and budget.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional quality furniture that enhances living and working spaces, delivered with outstanding customer service and value for money.

Our Vision

To be Kenya's most trusted and preferred furniture provider, known for quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
