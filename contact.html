Frequently Asked Questions

🚚 Do you deliver outside Nairobi? ▼

Yes, we offer nationwide delivery across Kenya. Delivery fees vary by location. Contact us for a quote.

⏱️ How long does delivery take? ▼

Within Nairobi: Same-day delivery (order before 3 PM). Delivery fee: KES 500 - KES 1,500 depending on location.
Outside Nairobi: 2-5 business days.

🔧 Do you offer assembly services? ▼

Yes, free assembly within Nairobi for all furniture purchases.

📝 What is your warranty policy? ▼

1-year warranty on all products against manufacturing defects.

🔄 Can I return a product? ▼

Yes, within 7 days of delivery if unused and in original packaging.

🛠️ Do you make custom furniture? ▼

Yes, we accept custom orders. Lead time is 10-14 days depending on the design.

💳 What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and cash on delivery (for select locations).
